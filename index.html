Thumble, Big Button Navigation in Stylus and CSS for Mobile and Touch

Don't you tell me you haven't fumbled around with your thumbs clicking on tiny buttons and menu items most web app widget libraries offer. Thumble, our PlaySkool™ size button-pad widget is designed not just for mobile-first, but touch-first. Browsing the web has beena screen-touching experience for most for more than a few years. It's time for a touch-centric approach to navigation that might just be universal.
